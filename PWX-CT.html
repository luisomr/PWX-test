About Precision Worx

Precision Worx is the result of a powerful merger uniting over 75 years of expertise in aerospace fasteners and precision manufacturing. Born from industry leaders in Connecticut’s aerospace corridor, we specialize in high-strength, high-temperature fasteners and custom components that meet the rigorous demands of aerospace, defense, medical, and industrial sectors. Our legacy of innovation, backed by patented technologies and a commitment to quality, ensures every fastener and part we produce drives performance and reliability.

Our Capabilities

Aerospace Fasteners

High-strength bolts, screws, and specialty fasteners engineered for aircraft and defense systems, meeting FAA and OEM standards.

Precision Machining

Advanced CNC machining for complex components, from micro-parts to large assemblies, with tight tolerances.

Custom Solutions

Tailored fasteners and components for unique applications, from prototyping to high-volume production.

Quality Assurance
